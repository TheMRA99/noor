PRIVACY POLICY · NOOR

Privacy.

EFFECTIVE 13 MAY 2026 · VERSION 1.0

Noor stores nothing about you off the device. No accounts. No analytics. No tracking. No advertising. No third parties.

IN ONE LINE

Your name, bookmarks, last-read position, theme, and remembrance progress live in your phone's local browser storage — and only there.

What we collect

Nothing. No name, no email, no phone, no location, no device identifier, no usage statistics — nothing leaves your device.

What we store on your device

Noor uses the browser/WebView's localStorage — a small, app-private store on your phone — to remember:

  • The name you chose to be greeted by (if you entered one)
  • Bookmarked verses
  • Last-read surah and ayah
  • Theme preference (dark / light)
  • Reciter preference and reader settings (font size, transliteration toggle)
  • Adhkār (morning / evening remembrance) progress for the current day

This data never leaves your device. If you uninstall Noor, it is deleted with the app. You can also tap Clear all data in the Profile page at any time to wipe it manually.

What network requests Noor makes

Noor makes anonymous, read-only requests to two public services to serve sacred content:

  • Quran.com API — for the verse text and translation. api.quran.com
  • EveryAyah — for the audio recitations. everyayah.com

These requests carry no identifying information — just the surah and verse being read. Standard server logs at those services may record your IP address; we have no access to those logs and do not collect or correlate them.

No analytics, no tracking

Noor includes no analytics SDKs, no fingerprinting, no advertising libraries, no crash reporters, no social plugins, no remote configuration. We do not know who you are, when you opened the app, or which verses you read — and that is the design.

Children's privacy

Noor is appropriate for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children — because we do not collect any data at all.

Permissions

On both Android and iOS, Noor requests no special permissions. It does not need access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, photos, or files. The only system facility it uses is local storage on the device itself.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change anything material about how the app handles data, this page will reflect the change and the version + effective date above will update. There is no email list to notify — there is no list.

Contact

For questions, open an issue at github.com/TheMRA99/noor/issues. The source code is public — you can read it line by line to verify everything on this page.
